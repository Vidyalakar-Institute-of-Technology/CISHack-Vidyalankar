Other Vaccines

Ebola Vaccine

Until very recently there was no licensed vaccine for Ebola. The most recent outbreak of Ebola in the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) seems to be nearly over, yet health officials are not standing down just yet.

MARBURG VIRUS Vaccine

In 2017, in the most recent outbreak in Uganda, there were 3 cases, all of whom died. A previous outbreak in 2005 in Angola resulted in over 200 infections, 90% of whom died.

LASSA FEVER Vaccine

Experts suspect there are between 100,000 and 300,000 infections of Lassa fever annually, with around 5,000 deaths, and limited evidence emerging of infections in previously non-endemic areas.

MERS-COV Vaccine

A MERS-CoV vaccine has gone as far as phase 2 clinical trials, though those candidates are now being tested for effectiveness against COVID-19.
